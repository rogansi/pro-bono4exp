More About Me

I am a lifelong resident of Minnesota with deep roots in the community and a strong love of beauty in all of its forms. It has always been a dream of mine to be able to create artwork that brings joy and comfort to people who need it.

My work can be seen here online as well as at Apricot Lane at its location at the shops in west end. I do custom work as well as professional craft work and makeup.
